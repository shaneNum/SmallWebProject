What Kind Of Mod Do I Like?

There's so much i like, but ill spare you the detail. I'll choose 2, and both have their own perks & aspect to their own respective game and style.

Minecraft: Create Mod

What if minecraft have much more cooler technology? But still feel to the core roots of its style? Well this is it, the most magical and by far the best minecraft mod for its innovation and creativity. this mod make minecraft farms much more interesting take, instead a block of multi pourpose factory, Create makes you create the factory to your liking, its so customizable to the point it feels like another game called "Factorio". This mod adds a lot, a fully customizable train, gears that make the factory move, and the core of power called rotation, that can be created using windmill, steam engine, waterwheel and so many more. And all of it can be design to your liking, making it easy to learn and hard to master for space, effectivity, and efficiency. if you want to know how good it is, click this title!

Terraria: Calamity Mod

The ultimate challenge, sooo many addition thats spice the vanilla Terraria and crank it to the hundreds! So many boss, so many weapon, so many progression, new class, battle overhaul, and beatiful art and music, and the difficulty are insane. I recommend to finish terraria first all of it before tackling this behemoth of a mod, so many cool thing created that even the dev couldnt even come up, with the lore & story revamp, wonders it made to follow foot to your journeys to over coming everything. But do note, you'll be downloading a lot of addons that corelate to Calamity Mod, like Calamity Music Mod, Catalyst Mod, Or If You Wanna Even Harder Calamity, Infernum Mod. If that havent peak your interest click the title to see what you're sign up to.
